Keywords

Keywords: Words or phrases that are used to match your ads with the terms people are searching for.

  • Be specific
  • Consider the terminolgy a customer vill be using
  • Group by themes

Organice the keywords by themes. Ex. Men's boots -> Men's rainboots, men's work boots, etc.

Google keyword planner

  1. Brainstorm inital ideas: What is your custmer thinking? What are they looking for? How would they search for it?
  2. Use Keyword planer to gather more ideas
  3. Review the data associated with each keyword.

Keyword Options

  • Broad match
  • Broad match modifier: add a "+" before the keyword
  • Phrase match: add quotes for the search term, most contain the excect phrase.
  • Exact: add brakets "[]"
  • Negative match: add a "-" before the keyword, remove the keyword from the search

Improve Keywords

  • Use negative keywords
  • Remove duplicate keywords
  • Optimize low-performing keywords (and remove then)

6 Tips for Effective Ads

  • Highlight what makes you unique. What makes you stand out? What is your unique selling point? What key feature does your product have?
  • Use a call to action.
  • Use sales teminiology like price information.
  • Match your ads to your keywords.
  • Match your ad to your landing page.
  • Experiment, create 3-4 ads for each ad group. AdWords will rotate them and favors the best ads.
